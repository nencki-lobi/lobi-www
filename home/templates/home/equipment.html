equipment


Scanner 3T Siemens TRIO equipped with:
• 32 channel head coil compatible with iPAT (32-Channel Head)
• 12 channel head coil compatible with iPAT (Head Matrix)
• Circular polarized transmit/receive coil for spectroscopy (Tx/Rx CP Head)

Following imaging methods are available:
• functional imaging (fMRI) based on BOLD contrast; hardware and software for presentation of visual and auditory stimuli
• brain morphology assessment MRI perfusion weighed imaging based on arterial spin labeling (ASL), cerebral blood flow (CBF), cerebral blood volume (CBV), mean transit time (MTT), i time-to-peak (TTP)
• diffusion tensor imaging (DTI) up to 256 directions, b=<10 000s/mm2 proton magnetic resonance spectroscopy (single-voxel and CSI/), angiography

Methods:
• Functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging (fMRI)
• Pattern Recognition and Multivariate methods for fMRI analysis
• Voxel-Based Morphometry
• Voxel-based Lesion Symptom Mapping
• Resting-State fMRI
• Diffusion tensor imaging (DTI; up to 256 directions, b≤10 000s/mm2)
• Proton magnetic resonance spectroscopy (single voxel and chemical shift imaging)
• Whole-brain chemical shift imaging*
• MEGA-PRESS (concentrations of neurotransmitters: GABA and glutamate)
• Perfusion (cerebral blood flow imaging)
• Angiography

Additional equipment:
• 64 channel EEG Brain Product system compatible with MRI scanner with electromyography (EMG) module and galvanic skin response (GSR) – more information about our system.
• Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) MagVenture system
• Brainsight TMS Navigation for MRI-based TMS Neuronavigation

• MRI Simulator (also known as mock scanner).
